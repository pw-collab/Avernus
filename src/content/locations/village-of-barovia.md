The Village of Barovia is the settlement most travellers reach first after the
Mists close behind them, and it sets the tone for the whole
[valley](/domains/barovia). It is a small, shuttered place of muddy streets and
locked doors, where the people keep their heads down, mistrust strangers, and live
in open dread of the ruler whose castle looms above them.

## A village in mourning

Grief hangs over the village like the permanent overcast. Its inhabitants speak
carefully, avoid the night, and treat outsiders as either doomed or dangerous. A
tavern, a church, and a handful of grim houses make up most of what a visitor
sees; behind their walls, families guard what little they have and wait out a fear
that never lifts.

## Why it matters

The village is where the valley's human cost is easiest to feel, and where several
threads of the story begin. It is the home of [Ireena Kolyana](/npcs/ireena-kolyana),
whose plight draws the lord's attention toward the village directly, and it is the
usual starting point from which newcomers begin to understand the trap they have
walked into.
