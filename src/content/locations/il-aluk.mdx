Il Aluk is the principal city of [Darkon](/domains/darkon) and the seat of its
lich-king, [Azalin Rex](/darklords/azalin-rex). For most of its history it is
exactly what a great capital should be: large, busy, and cosmopolitan — a place
where the domain's quiet curse of forgetting is easy to overlook amid ordinary
city life.

## A city of two fates

Il Aluk is also a clear example of how the setting's canon shifts across editions.
In one edition a disaster overtakes the city and transforms it into a realm of
the dead, sealed behind a lethal fog; in others it remains a living capital. Both
versions are recorded here rather than reconciled, since which one is "true"
depends entirely on the edition a reader is playing.
