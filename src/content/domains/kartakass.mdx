Kartakass is a highland domain of deep forests and scattered villages whose
people love a good song and a good story above all. Talent is currency here, and
the finest performers are treated as local royalty — which is precisely the
weakness its ruler exploits.

## Charm as a weapon

The domain's horror hides behind conviviality. Beneath the festivals and the fine
voices is a land ruled by a creature that uses charm the way others use claws.
Kartakass is a study in ambition dressed as artistry, where admiration is a trap
and the applause never quite ends.

## The performer-king

Kartakass belongs to [Harkon Lukas](/darklords/harkon-lukas), a wolfwere whose
gifts as an entertainer are matched only by his hunger to rule — a hunger the
Mists ensure he can never fully satisfy.
