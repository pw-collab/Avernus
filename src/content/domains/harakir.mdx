Har'Akir is one of the setting's Islands of Terror — a domain set apart from the
central lands, wrapped in desert rather than joined to the Core. It is a place of
sand-choked ruins, forgotten tombs, and a civilization that remembers being far
greater than it is. Its horror is the horror of the ancient curse: the past
refusing to stay buried.

## The desert that does not release

The dunes and storms of Har'Akir are barrier enough that the Mists rarely need to
show themselves. Travellers lose their bearings among identical horizons, and the
tombs beneath the sand hold guardians that were laid to rest with no intention of
staying there.

## The undying priest

Har'Akir answers to [Ankhtepot](/darklords/ankhtepot), a priest-king who cannot
die and cannot rest, and whose refusal to release either his people or himself
keeps the whole domain locked in its long decline.
