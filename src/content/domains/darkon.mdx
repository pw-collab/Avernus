Darkon is one of the largest and most peopled of the core Domains of Dread,
ruled for ages by the lich [Azalin Rex](/darklords/azalin-rex). Where
[Barovia](/domains/barovia) is a claustrophobic valley, Darkon is broad and
outwardly ordinary — which is part of its horror. Its danger is patient and
quiet: the domain slowly rewrites the pasts of those who settle in it, so that
newcomers forget where they came from and come to belong to Darkon alone.

## A kingdom that eats memory

The realm's signature curse is subtle. Travellers who linger find their
recollections of home thinning until they can no longer say who they once were.
A population assembled this way is docile and rootless, which suits a ruler who
prizes order and study above all.

## The wizard's long rivalry

Azalin's realm is often set against Strahd's: two undying, intelligent lords,
each a prisoner of a different appetite. Their mutual contempt — the scholar
against the conqueror — is one of the setting's enduring rivalries, and a
convenient hook for stories that reach beyond a single domain.

## The capital

The seat of Darkon's power is the great city of [Il Aluk](/locations/il-aluk),
whose changing fortunes across editions are noted above.
