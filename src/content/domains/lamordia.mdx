Lamordia is a cold northern domain of glaciers, rocky coasts, and orderly towns
that pride themselves on reason. Its people are rationalists who trust in science
and dismiss the supernatural — a confidence that leaves them badly prepared for
the very real horror in their midst.

## The terror of the made thing

The domain's defining story is one of creation gone wrong: a brilliant, isolated
scientist and the living being assembled by his hands. It is the setting's clear
homage to the Frankenstein tradition, and its horror is intimate rather than
grand — a matter of hubris, abandonment, and a creature that only wanted to be
wanted.

## The maker and the made

Two figures embody Lamordia's tragedy: the scientist
[Victor Mordenheim](/npcs/victor-mordenheim) and his creation,
[Adam](/darklords/adam). Which of them is the true prisoner of the domain has
been answered differently across editions, as noted above.
