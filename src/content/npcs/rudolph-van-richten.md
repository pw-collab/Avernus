Rudolph van Richten is the setting's foremost hunter of monsters: a learned,
weary man who turned to the study and destruction of the undead after personal
tragedy set him on that road. Where many of the setting's figures are victims or
villains, he is one of its few genuine agents of resistance — an expert whose
notes and methods have equipped countless others to survive.

## The scholar-hunter

Van Richten's value is knowledge. He has spent a lifetime cataloguing how the
setting's horrors think, feed, and can be opposed, and his guides to hunting them
are treated in-setting as essential reading. He tends to enter a story as a
mentor: someone who can tell newcomers what they are truly facing and what it will
cost to face it.

## In the valley

Within [Barovia](/domains/barovia) his expertise turns naturally against
[Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich), the archetypal vampire the
whole setting revolves around. He is wary of easy hope and blunt about the price
of confronting such a creature, which makes him a grounding presence in a place
built to wear its visitors down.
