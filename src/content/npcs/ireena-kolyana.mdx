Ireena Kolyana is a young woman of the [Village of Barovia](/locations/village-of-barovia),
raised by the village's priest, whose life is upended when the valley's ruler
turns his attention on her. She is capable and strong-willed rather than a passive
victim, but she is caught in a situation far older and larger than herself.

## The lord's attention

[Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich) pursues Ireena with the
patience of someone who believes she already belongs to him. His interest brings
danger not only to her but to everyone around her, and protecting her tends to be
one of the first purposes newcomers to the valley adopt — which is precisely how
the lord draws them into his orbit.

<SpoilerWrapper level="dm-only" summary="Who Ireena really is">

Ireena is the current reincarnation of the woman the lord loved and lost when he
first damned himself. She is reborn in each generation wearing that same face, and
the lord is compelled to pursue her every time, only to lose her again — the cycle
that keeps his tragedy turning. Handled well, her story is less a rescue mission
than a slow revelation of a trap that has sprung many times before.
