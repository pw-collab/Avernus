Madam Eva is an old and uncannily knowing seer who camps with her people near the
edges of [Barovia](/domains/barovia). She is best known for the fortune she tells
newcomers: a reading of cards that names where certain aids are hidden, who might
stand with the travellers, and where the confrontation with the valley's ruler
should ultimately fall.

## The reading

The card reading is the setting's signature device. Because the seer's fortune
randomizes those key locations and allies each time it is performed, no two
journeys through the valley unfold the same way — the same mechanic that made the
original adventure replayable, carried forward into its modern form. For a game
master, her reading is the tool that seeds a whole campaign in a single scene.

## Her place in the valley

Madam Eva's exact relationship to [Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich)
is deliberately left shadowed: she is neither plainly his servant nor plainly his
enemy, and different tellings weight that ambiguity differently. What is constant
is her role as the story's oracle — the voice that tells arrivals what shape their
doom, or their escape, might take.
