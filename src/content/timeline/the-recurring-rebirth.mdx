<SpoilerWrapper level="dm-only" summary="Why it keeps happening">

The woman Strahd lost is reborn in each generation wearing the same face — most
recently [Ireena Kolyana](/npcs/ireena-kolyana). He is compelled to pursue her
every time and to fail every time, which is the engine that keeps his punishment
turning. A story set in the valley is, at bottom, one more turn of this wheel.
