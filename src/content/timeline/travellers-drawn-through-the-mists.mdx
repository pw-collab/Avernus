The usual beginning of a story in [Barovia](/domains/barovia): newcomers stumble
through the Mists and cannot get out again. Near the border,
[Madam Eva](/npcs/madam-eva) reads their fortune, seeding where help is hidden,
who might stand with them, and where the reckoning with the lord should fall.
