<SpoilerWrapper level="dm-only" summary="What the bargain took">

Consumed by envy and by a desire that was never returned, Strahd shed family
blood to seize immortality. The power was real and the price immediate: the
woman he wanted was lost to him in the same moment, and the deal remade him into
something that could not die and could not leave. Every later sorrow in
[Barovia](/domains/barovia) descends from this night.

</SpoilerWrapper>
