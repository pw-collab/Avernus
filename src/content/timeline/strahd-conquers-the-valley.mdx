[Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich) wins
[Barovia](/domains/barovia) as a soldier, ending years of war and claiming the
castle on the crag as his seat. It is the high point of his mortal life — and,
in hindsight, the last moment before everything he wanted began to slip away.
