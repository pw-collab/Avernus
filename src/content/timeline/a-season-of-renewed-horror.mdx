This entry marks a real-world milestone rather than an in-world one: the 2026
sourcebook that recollects the setting for the current edition. For an archive
like this it is the most recent canonical touchstone, and where its account of
[Barovia](/domains/barovia) differs from older editions, those differences are
recorded as edition notes rather than overwriting what came before.
