Azalin Rex is the lich-king of [Darkon](/domains/darkon), and one of the
setting's most important darklords after the lord of
[Barovia](/domains/barovia). He is a scholar and tyrant in equal measure: a
mind of enormous patience and cruelty who treats his whole realm as an
instrument of study.

## The scholar's torment

Where the vampire of Barovia is trapped by longing, Azalin is trapped by
ambition. He craves mastery — of magic, of the Mists, of the terms of his own
imprisonment — and is perpetually denied completion. His curse turns his
greatest strength, relentless intellect, into the engine of his suffering: he
learns and schemes forever and is never satisfied.

## The rival

Azalin and [Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich) are the classic
opposed pair of the setting — the wizard and the warlord, cold calculation
against hot pride. Their rivalry has spanned editions and makes a natural spine
for any story that crosses the borders between their domains.
