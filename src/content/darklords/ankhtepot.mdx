Ankhtepot is the darklord of [Har'Akir](/domains/harakir), a mummified priest-king
who rules a desert full of tombs — one of which is meant to be his own. He is the
setting's classic mummy villain: patient, ancient, and bound to a faith and a
land that will not let him go.

## The rest he cannot have

Ankhtepot's torment is a bitter inversion. In life he refused to accept death and
took terrible measures to escape it; in undeath he is denied the true rest he
ultimately came to want. He rises night after night, deathless and weary, ruling
a kingdom that is functionally a grave.

## Guardian of the old ways

He clings to the rites and hierarchy of a civilization long past its height, and
much of Har'Akir's danger comes from his insistence that the old order still
holds. To disturb the tombs is to answer to him.
