Adam is the created being at the centre of [Lamordia](/domains/lamordia)'s
tragedy — the setting's answer to the Frankenstein monster. He is enormously
strong and effectively deathless, and he is desperately, ruinously alone.

## Rejected by his maker

Adam was assembled and animated by the scientist
[Victor Mordenheim](/npcs/victor-mordenheim), who then turned from him in horror.
That abandonment is the wound the whole domain turns on. Adam's strength cannot
buy him the one thing he wants — to be accepted, or to be given a companion of
his own kind — and his attempts to force that outcome only deepen the tragedy.

## A curse of loneliness

Where other darklords are undone by pride or ambition, Adam is undone by a need
for connection he can neither earn nor abandon. It makes him one of the setting's
more sympathetic monsters: a creature whose cruelty is inseparable from his
loneliness.
