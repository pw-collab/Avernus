Harkon Lukas is the darklord of [Kartakass](/domains/kartakass), a wolfwere who
has made himself the most celebrated performer in his domain and intends to make
himself far more. He is charming, cultured, and genuinely gifted — and entirely
predatory beneath it.

## The ambition that cages him

Harkon's curse is scaled to his ego. He dreams of ruling nations and commanding
loyalty on a grand stage, but the Mists keep him lord of a modest realm of forest
villages. Every scheme to expand his reach is quietly, permanently frustrated, so
that his ambition becomes a treadmill he can never step off.

## Charm and menace

He works best as a villain who is a pleasure to be around — a host, a mentor, a
star — right up until the moment the beast shows. In a domain that prizes
performance, his greatest weapon is simply how much everyone wants to believe in
him.
