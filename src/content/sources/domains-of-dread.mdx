This second-edition hardcover gathered the setting's many domains and their
cursed rulers into one systematic reference. It codified the structure the
setting is known for — realms defined by their darklord's crime and bounded by
the Mists — and catalogued a broad roster of domains that later editions would
revisit and revise.

For this archive it serves as the foundational older-edition citation: where a
domain or darklord's earlier characterization differs from the modern one, this
is frequently the source being compared against.
