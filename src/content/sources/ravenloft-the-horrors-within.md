This 2026 sourcebook is a broad reference for gothic-horror play under the
current edition's rules. Rather than a single adventure, it collects setting
material — an overview of the misty prison-realms, the tormented figures who rule
them, and guidance for building horror-flavoured stories and characters.

It is positioned as an entry point for a renewed wave of interest in the setting,
released alongside supporting accessories. For an archive like this one it serves
as the most recent canonical touchstone: where its presentation of a domain or
character differs from earlier editions, those differences are recorded here as
edition-specific notes rather than overwriting the older material.
