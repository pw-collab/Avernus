The System Reference Document collects the portion of the game's rules released
for open reuse — under the fifth edition, via a Creative Commons license. It
covers generic mechanics (conditions, core actions, and the like) and pointedly
**excludes** setting-specific intellectual property.

This distinction is the backbone of the archive's legal approach: mechanics that
appear here are safe to reproduce, while anything unique to Ravenloft — its
domains, darklords, and its bespoke horror subsystems — is not covered and must
be described in original words only. The **Mechanics** collection flags each
entry as SRD-safe or WotC-proprietary against exactly this line.
