This fifth-edition campaign adventure reimagines the original haunted-valley
scenario at book length. It carries a party from an ill-fated arrival through the
Mists to a climactic confrontation with the domain's ruler, expanding the small
1983 module into a sprawling, sandbox-style region full of side plots,
settlements, and factions.

The adventure revives the card-reading mechanic of its predecessor: a fortune
told at the outset seeds the campaign with randomized goals, allies, and the
location where the final confrontation should take place, so each group's story
differs. It also fleshes out the valley's towns and the people caught inside its
borders, giving the setting a lived-in texture the terse original only implied.

Widely credited with renewing mainstream interest in gothic-horror play for the
game, it is the most common entry point through which modern audiences encounter
the setting.
