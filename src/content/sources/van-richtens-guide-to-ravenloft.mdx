This fifth-edition guide reframed the setting as a flexible toolbox rather than a
fixed map. It presents a curated gallery of Domains of Dread — each a
self-contained horror premise with its darklord — alongside guidance for running
fear, dread, and mystery at the table and for designing new domains of one's own.

Its emphasis on horror genres over a single continuous geography made it the
modern entry point for exploring the setting beyond its best-known valley, and it
is the primary fifth-edition citation for many of the domains catalogued here.
