This is the mechanical heart of horror in the setting: a framework for testing
whether characters keep their nerve when confronted with the frightening, the
gruesome, and the sanity-shaking. The intent is to make fear a resource the
players actually manage rather than a mood the narration merely asserts.

Because this subsystem is specific to the setting's intellectual property, this
archive describes it **in original words only** and does not reproduce its rules
text — see the legal flag on this entry. Contributors adapting it for play should
consult the cited books directly.
