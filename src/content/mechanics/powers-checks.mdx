A powers check is what happens, mechanically, when a character does something
genuinely wicked within the Domains of Dread. Rather than a simple morality
tally, it models the unseen Dark Powers taking an interest: the act may be
"rewarded" with a creeping gift that corrupts the recipient over time. It is the
rules-level engine of how ordinary people slide toward becoming monsters — the
same road every darklord once walked.

This subsystem is specific to the setting and is described here **in original
words only**, per the legal flag on this entry.
