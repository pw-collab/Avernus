The frightened condition is a generic status effect: a frightened creature is
hampered while the source of its fear remains in sight, and cannot willingly move
closer to that source. Unlike the setting's dedicated horror subsystem, this
condition is **generic rules content** released for open reuse, so it may be
reproduced and adapted freely — which is why this entry is flagged **SRD-safe**
rather than WotC-proprietary.

Its inclusion here is deliberate: it shows the line the archive's Mechanics
collection draws. Generic scaffolding like this is fair game; the setting-specific
[Fear, Horror, and Madness](/mechanics/fear-horror-and-madness) rules layered on
top of it are not, and are only ever described in original words.
