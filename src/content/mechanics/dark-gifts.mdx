A dark gift is a boon handed out by the setting's unseen Dark Powers — an
ability or advantage that is real and useful and always carries a hidden cost. It
is the tempting side of corruption: the mechanism by which the domains reward the
very behaviour that damns their inhabitants. Every darklord is, in a sense, the
end state of accepting too many such gifts.

This is setting-specific material, described here **in original words only** per
the legal flag on this entry.
