The Tome of Strahd is the private record of
[Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich) — his own telling of his
rise, his ruinous bargain, and the sorrow beneath his cruelty. For those trapped
in [Barovia](/domains/barovia), it is the most reliable window into the lord's
nature, and reading it is often the moment a story turns from surviving him to
understanding him.

## A key, not a blade

The Tome's power is knowledge. It does not strike at the lord directly; it makes
plain who he is and what he fears, which in the right hands is worth more than
any sword. It is one of the fated treasures a fortune-teller's reading may point
newcomers toward.
