The Sunsword is a blade of light and one of the three fated treasures a
fortune-teller's reading may set newcomers to find in [Barovia](/domains/barovia).
Its radiance is anathema to creatures of the night, which makes it one of the few
things in the valley that [Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich)
has cause to dread.

## A weapon against the night

Where the [Tome of Strahd](/artifacts/tome-of-strahd) offers understanding and a
holy relic offers protection, the Sunsword offers force — a means of actually
ending the threat rather than merely surviving it. Its exact location is not
fixed: the card reading tied to the valley scatters where such treasures wait.
