The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind is a consecrated relic and the third of the fated
treasures a fortune-teller's reading may direct newcomers toward in
[Barovia](/domains/barovia). In a valley where faith is strained and the church
beleaguered, it is both a spiritual anchor and a practical defence against
[Strahd von Zarovich](/darklords/strahd-von-zarovich) and his servants.

## Faith as a weapon

Together with the [Sunsword](/artifacts/the-sunsword) and the
[Tome of Strahd](/artifacts/tome-of-strahd), the Holy Symbol completes the trio
of aids the valley's fortune may promise: protection and blessing to set beside
the sword's force and the tome's insight. It is most closely tied to the
struggling faithful of the [Village of Barovia](/locations/village-of-barovia).
